Google Ads for Law Firms: PPC That Converts

Expert SEO resources for law firms

The Law Firm Google Ads Advantage

Google Ads (formerly AdWords) puts your firm in front of high-intent searchers immediately—no waiting for organic rankings. When someone searches "personal injury attorney," you can appear at the top within hours. For competitive practice areas, paid search complements SEO perfectly.

Building Your Law Firm Ad Account

Structure campaigns by practice area or service type. Create separate campaigns for DUI, personal injury, family law, etc. Within each campaign, organize ad groups by keyword theme. This structure ensures relevant ads show for specific searches and improves Quality Score.

Keyword Strategy for Legal Services

Use high-intent keywords: "hire DUI attorney," "personal injury lawyer consultation," "divorce attorney near me." Avoid broad match unless you want volume; use phrase and exact match to control costs. Negative keywords are critical—exclude queries like "DIY," "free," and competitor names you don't want.

Writing Winning Ad Copy

Your headline must match the search intent. Include your location and practice area. Use numbers when possible: "30+ years experience," "90% settlement rate." Address objections: "Free consultation," "No upfront fees," "Confidential review." Include a clear call-to-action: "Call now," "Schedule free consultation."

Landing Page Optimization

Never send Google Ads traffic to your homepage. Create specific landing pages matching each ad group. Page should match the ad promise, load instantly, have a prominent CTA, minimal form fields, and clear law firm credentials.

Tracking & Cost Management

Set up conversion tracking for calls, form submissions, and chat inquiries. Calculate your true cost-per-lead by tracking which leads convert to clients. Monitor your quality score—higher scores mean lower costs. Start with generous budgets, then optimize based on performance data.
