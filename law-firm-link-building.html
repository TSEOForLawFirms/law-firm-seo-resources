Law Firm Link Building Guide

Expert SEO resources for law firms

Why Backlinks Matter for Law Firm Rankings

Google treats backlinks as votes of confidence. When other websites link to you, you gain authority. For competitive legal markets, backlinks are often the difference between ranking on page one and page ten. Strategic link building is essential for law firm SEO success.

Legal Directory Submissions

The easiest links come from legal directories. Get listed on Avvo, Google Scholar, Super Lawyers, Best Lawyers, and practice-area-specific directories. These sites have high authority and are trusted by search engines. Some require fees; some are free. Even basic listings provide valuable links.

Bar Association Links

Your state and local bar associations should link to your firm. State bar lawyer directories provide high-authority links. Local bar association membership pages link to member sites. These links are highly relevant and trusted by search engines.

Local Business Listings

Get cited and linked from local business sites: Chamber of Commerce, Better Business Bureau, local news sites, and city directories. These local links boost your geographic authority and help you rank in local search results.

Earning Editorial Links

The best links come naturally when journalists and bloggers reference your firm. Build relationships with legal reporters. Pitch yourself as an expert on your practice area. When news breaks related to your specialty, journalists should think of you for comment.

Guest Posting & Content Partnerships

Write guest posts for established legal blogs and legal industry publications. Each guest post includes a link back to your site. Partner with complementary services (accountants, financial planners) for mutual linking. Create original research or reports that others naturally want to link to.
