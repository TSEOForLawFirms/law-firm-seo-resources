The Best Free Tools in Your SEO Arsenal

Effective SEO doesn't require expensive software. Free tools give law firms visibility into their SEO performance, competitor strategies, and optimization opportunities. Master these tools and you'll have insights most competitors don't.

Google Search Console

This is essential. Search Console shows you which searches bring traffic, which pages rank, your click-through rate, and crawl errors. It's the most important free tool for any law firm website. Set it up immediately and check it weekly.

Google Analytics 4

Analytics shows what happens after someone lands on your site. Track which pages attract traffic, how long visitors stay, which traffic sources convert best, and which practice area pages perform strongest. Use this data to improve your highest-value pages.

Google Business Profile

This is free and critical for local SEO. Manage your Google Business Profile directly in Google's platform. Post updates, respond to reviews, and see which searches bring you phone calls and website clicks.

Ubersuggest (Free Version)

Ubersuggest's free version shows keyword search volume and difficulty. Research keywords for your practice areas. Understand which keywords are easiest to rank for initially and which are worth long-term effort.

Answer the Public

This tool visualizes questions people ask about your practice areas. See the exact questions prospective clients search. Use this to create FAQ pages and blog content directly answering what your ideal clients want to know.

MozBar

The free Mozbar browser extension shows page authority and domain authority scores while you browse. Analyze competitor sites to understand what makes them rank. Check your own pages to identify authority gaps.
