Local SEO for Attorneys: Google Maps Domination

Expert SEO resources for law firms

Why Local SEO is Critical for Law Firms

Most potential clients search "lawyer near me" or specify their location. Local SEO ensures your firm appears in Google Maps, local pack results, and location-specific organic listings. This is where most law firm clients start their search.

Google Business Profile Optimization

Your GBP is the foundation of local SEO. Claim and verify your listing immediately if you haven't. Complete every field: full address, phone number, website, hours, practice areas. Add 50+ high-quality photos: office interior, team, client testimonials, before/after documentation. Post weekly updates about practice areas, tips, and case results.

Local Citation Building

Citations are online mentions of your firm's name, address, and phone number. Build them on legal directories (Avvo, SuperLawyers, Best Lawyers), local business sites (BBB, Yelp), and practice-area-specific listings. Consistency is crucial—ensure NAP matches everywhere.

Review Generation & Management

Reviews are ranking factors and trust signals. Encourage satisfied clients to leave Google reviews (they're most valuable). Respond to all reviews—thank positive reviewers and professionally address negative ones. More recent reviews carry more weight.

Local Link Building

Earn links from local chamber of commerce, bar associations, legal aid organizations, and local news sites. Sponsor community events, donate to local charities, and get mentioned in local publications. These local links boost geographic authority.

Service Area Pages

Create dedicated pages for each service area you serve (different cities/counties). Include local keywords, local backlinks, and location-specific information. For multi-office firms, maintain a separate GBP for each location.
